Rules of Conduct for Fin's Kingdom

1. No NSFW Jokes or Media

Avoid making any jokes or comments of any sexual nature.

  • Absolutely refrain from making jokes related to rape or any form of sexual violence.
  • Any NSFW posted will get you a banned ⚠️
  • No jokes about pedophilia, or fake accusations
  • No homophobic or transphobic comments
  • No excessive use of the N word
  • Any comments regarding H*tler will get you banned/kicked

2. Respect Family

Do not bring up or involve anyone's family members in arguments.

  • Avoid making comments about someone's family, whether positive or negative, in contentious discussions.

3. Respect Religion

Do not involve or reference anyone's religious beliefs in arguments.

  • Avoid making derogatory comments about any religion or religious practices.

4. General Respect

Treat everyone with respect, regardless of their background or beliefs.

Focus on the topic at hand rather than personal attributes or affiliations.

5. Respect the Privacy of Others

Do not share personal information of other members without their consent. Respect the privacy of all users.

6. Stay in the right channel

Make sure you post messages in the appropriate channels. Avoid off-topic discussions in the wrong channels.

7. Consequences

Be aware that violating these rules can result in disciplinary action, including but not limited to warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the community.

8. Listen to Staff

Respect and Follow Staff Instructions:

Always listen to and follow the instructions given by staff members. If you have concerns or disagreements, address them politely through appropriate channels.

These rules are in place to ensure a respectful and safe environment is placed for everyone.
